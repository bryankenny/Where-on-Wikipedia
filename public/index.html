    One of history's greatest villains has set their sights on Wikipedia and stolen an article!

    Put your sleuthing skills to the test and capture them before they escape and the article disappears from Wikipedia's servers forever! Disclaimer: No Wikipedia articles were harmed in the making of this game.

    How To Play

    As the game starts, you'll be presented with a Wikipedia article - the last-known location of the thief. By searching for clues, doing some research, and examining the articles they may have gone to next you should be able to stay on their trail.

    Due to technical limitations, the game isn't able to track your movements directly as you click in the embedded Wikipedia window. For doing research, you can use the embedded window or open new browser tabs - regardless, travel must be done via the game interface.

    Click the button to start playing.

    Getting A Warrant

    Once you've learned enough about your suspect to identify them, enter their page ID (we're working on a better solution) in the Suspect tab and click the button to request a warrant. Be careful - when you catch up to the suspect, you'll need to have a warrant matching their identity or the Wikipedia police (I swear that's a real thing) will have to let them go.

    Capture

    When you finally catch up to the thief, you'll be given a warning that they're somewhere in the vicinity. Make sure you have a warrant, then search for clues one last time to unleash the local constabulary and bring your quarry to justice.
